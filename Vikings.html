Vikings Review

Pros:

  • A lot of selections of international Cuisine
  • Complete from Soups, Appetizers, Main Dishes, and Desserts!
  • International Flavour is everywhere!
  • Food is faithfully cooked from foreign recipes
  • You get to eat for free if it's your Birthday!

Cons:

  • Per Head price for the buffet is Expensive
  • Said price is even more expensive during weekdays!
  • If your tongue is not prepared for some foreign cuisine,
    you're in for a ride

Overall take:

Vikings is a luxury buffet very suitable for grand occassions. The ambiance, the professionality of the staff, as well as the taste of all their dishes speak for itself. Vikings will make your mouth water for the myriad of international cuisine that they display! Even better, if it's your birthday and you chose to dine with them, they will give you free cake, along with the staff dancing along to the Happy Birthday song, as they make you feel as exceptional as you can be, on your special day.

South Point, SM City Iloilo, BS, Benigno Aquino Ave, Iloilo City, Iloilo
